FAQ

Why do I have to sign up?
Actually, you don't need to sign up as you can do it with your facebook or twitter login. But to take advantage of advanced and cool features, we recommend you to sign up. Just take few seconds ;)
Should I provide my accurate location when using the app?
That is not necessary but it is recommendable. We know some users are reserved for their location, but with this feature we will provide you more accurate service and you won't waste time searching for results near you, as they will be provided by default.
